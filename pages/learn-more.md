# Campus Lending Program at Bryn Mawr College  
## Borrowing Artwork
### Art for Non-Public Spaces on Campus 

Faculty and Staff interested in borrowing artwork for their offices or other non-public spaces should browse this website for available artworks from the Circulating Art Loan Collection. Please be aware that artwork availability is in a constant state of flux due to office renovations, moves, and an ever-changing campus population.  
 

Once you have selected the artwork you wish to request, email [SpecColl@brynmawr.edu](mailto:speccoll@brynmawr.edu) with a link to the object as well as some of its descriptive information. Once requested, you will receive confirmation of its availability. The artwork will then be framed and readied for pick up. Please allow 6-8 weeks for this process.  
 

Costs involved in framing or displaying selected artworks may be the responsibility of the borrower. 

### Art for Public Spaces on Campus
Community members interested in borrowing artwork for public areas on campus should email [SpecColl@brynmawr.edu](mailto:speccoll@brynmawr.edu) for consultation. Public areas are considered hallways and common or unlocked areas, as well as rooms available to reserve through the College’s Conferences and Events EMS system. Some semi-public areas, such as conference rooms that can be made available for classes or researchers, will also be considered.  

 

Costs involved in framing or displaying selected artworks may be shared with Special Collections.  

## Returning Artwork 
Please email [SpecColl@brynmawr.edu](mailto:speccoll@brynmawr.edu) to initiate returns.
